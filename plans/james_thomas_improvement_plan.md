## Implementation Approach

### Website Development Options:

1. **DIY Approach**:
   - Use a static site generator like Eleventy, Hugo, or Next.js
   - Host on GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel for free/low-cost hosting
   - Maintain full control over design and implementation

2. **Template-Based Approach**:
   - Use a portfolio website builder (Squarespace, Wix, etc.)
   - Customize pre-built templates to match your requirements
   - Faster implementation but less customization

3. **Hybrid Approach**:
   - Start with a template/framework for basic structure
   - Customize key components to showcase your technical skills
   - Balance between speed and personalization

### Resume Development Options:

1. **Professional Resume Service**:
   - Work with a professional resume writer
   - Get expert guidance on content and structure
   - Higher cost but potentially better results

2. **DIY with Templates**:
   - Use professional resume templates (Canva, Resume.io, etc.)
   - Customize content while maintaining professional design
   - Balance between cost and quality

3. **Custom Design**:
   - Create a unique resume design that showcases your creative skills
   - Ensure it still meets professional standards and ATS requirements
   - Demonstrates your design capabilities
